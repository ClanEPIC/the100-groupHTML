EPIC CODE OF CONDUCT

In order to ensure that all EPIC members enjoy a pleasant experience, we expect that all act in a friendly and mature manner at all times, either when playing in games with EPIC members, or playing elsewhere if you have one of the EPIC clan tags on your in-game emblem. Rage quitters, those who have a constant, negative attitude or those who make negative comments in regards to race, sexual preference or religious affiliation will not be tolerated. Harassment or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated, whether in game chat or messages. Of course, it should go without saying that any felonious activity is also against the Code of Conduct and will not be tolerated.

If you can't make a the100.io game that you've signed up for, please remove yourself from the activity ASAP. If you are the game host/creator and can't make it, leave a note in game room chat letting people know the host has left so a new host can be appointed. Or delete the session if it does not have enough players. Make sure you're online at game time even if you are a reserve.

As a general rule, group membership is limited to those 18 years of age or older. There are exceptions though and if you are under 18, you are limited to playing games with your EPIC parent or guardian and may not post EPIC games on the100.io. Please ensure that your age is set and displayed on your the100.io profile. If you join another member's EPIC game, make sure you or your parent notify the game participants of your age. If you play with one of our under 18 members, remember your responsibilities as an adult. Treat our youth with respect and please insure that the environment is mature and cordial.

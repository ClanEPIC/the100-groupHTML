EPIC MISSION


Who is EPIC, and what makes us special?
We dont care if you have never raided or have hundreds of completions. We dont care if your crucible K/D is 0.3 or 3.3. EPIC members act mature, friendly, and helpful at all times. Our patient sherpas or streamers are willing to teach anyone that wants to learn raids or crucible skills. Our only requirement is that once you learn, pass it on to others! We are primarally a Destiny based clan but many of our members play other games together. If this sounds like you, welcome home Guardian!

WANT TO JOIN EPIC?

Step 1: click the "Join Group" button at the top of this page

Step 2: Join this game and say "hi" in the chat. Check back in that games chat from time to time as one one of our moderators will reach out to you.

Step 3: Carefully read and understand the EPIC CODE OF CONDUCT, below:

In order to ensure that all EPIC members enjoy a pleasant experience, we expect that all act in a friendly and mature manner at all times, either when playing in games with EPIC members, or playing elsewhere if you have one of the EPIC clan tags on your in-game emblem. Rage quitters, those who have a constant, negative attitude or those who make negative comments in regards to race, sexual preference or religious affiliation will not be tolerated. Harassment or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated, whether in game chat or messages. Of course, it should go without saying that any felonious activity is also against the Code of Conduct and will not be tolerated.

If you can't make a the100.io game that you've signed up for, please remove yourself from the activity ASAP. Make sure you're online at game time even if you are a reserve. If you are the game host/creator and can't make it, leave a note in game room chat letting people know the host has left so a new host can be appointed. Or delete the session if it does not have enough players. Invite players in the order that they signed up for the game. Do not skip people because a friend or someone you play with frequently is further down the list. If you have enough reserves to make up several teams, pulling from the list out of order is ok as long as you are just balancing the teams.

As a general rule, group membership is limited to those 18 years of age or older. There are exceptions though and if you are under 18, you are limited to playing games with your EPIC parent or guardian and may not post EPIC games on the100.io. Please ensure that your age is set and displayed on your the100.io profile. If you join another member's EPIC game, make sure you or your parent notify the game participants of your age. If you play with one of our under 18 members, remember your responsibilities as an adult. Treat our youth with respect and please ensure that the environment is mature and cordial.
Step 4, Optional: If you are approved for membership, our mods will provide you a link to our very active Discord. If you new to Discord, have a look at this short video from our very own ACBtheMan on how to use Discord

Step 5, Optional: If you would like to be a member of an EPIC clan, let our mods know and they will assign you to one. See links above.
